Merchant Verification

Welcome! You can send and receive payments using a variety of payment channels using our APIs in dozens of different currencies and nations. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Create a Nettmerchant account, if you haven't already
  2. Upload the Company and Business Documents.
  3. Fill the Bank Details
  4. When your company’s documents are verified you will be moved to live api keys
  5. If you want to collect payments from customers, visit the Collecting Payments overview.
  6. If you want to send money, visit the Making Payments overview.
  7. Build your integration, using one of our libraries or plugins.
